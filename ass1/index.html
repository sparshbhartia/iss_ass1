EDUCATION

Introduction:
My educational journey began at Delhi Public School (DPS), where I laid the foundation for academic excellence and holistic development from preschool to class 10. DPS provided a nurturing environment and diverse learning experiences, shaping my early years.
Transition to FIITJEE:
In pursuit of specialized preparation for competitive exams, I transitioned to FIITJEE for my 11th and 12th grades. This move marked a shift to a focused and rigorous learning environment, where I benefited from expert faculty, structured curriculum, and a competitive atmosphere.
Conclusion:
From DPS to FIITJEE, my educational journey has been transformative. The foundation laid at DPS, coupled with the focused preparation at FIITJEE, has equipped me with the skills, discipline, and knowledge needed for the challenges of higher education and competitive exams.
Going to IIITH:
My academic journey has led me to the International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad (IIIT-H). The journey from school to FIITJEE and now to IIIT-H has been a continuous exploration, shaping me into a student ready to make a meaningful impact in the world of technology.
